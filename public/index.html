Creating Team Principles

Sat, Dec 16, 2017 Canopy is growing very fast. That means we are hiring fast. And this means we have a greater risk of losing our way and our values as a team. Adding team members at a rapid pace can quickly alter the way a team behaves if one or two of those members don’t align to current values, behaviors and standards… have you ever heard the phrase “herding cats?” We all have a set of personal values whether we can define them or not.
