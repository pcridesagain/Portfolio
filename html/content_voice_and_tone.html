Voice and Tone

Good writing gets out of its own way.

In keeping with our mission of products that make practitioners lives easier and more efficient we have defined a voice profile that matches. The Canopy voice is self-aware, fresh, warm, assured, and perceptive.

Smart but not stuffy

We know all the ins and outs of accounting tax work and we can explain it in the most interesting way possible. We are the Neil deGrasse Tyson of taxes.

Confident but not arrogant

We’re confident that we have an amazing product, but we never push down our competition. Watch how Elon Musk talks about his products.

Empathetic but not dreary

We understand that accounting can really suck at times. We’re willing to sit next to you and listen to your ranting and validate you, but we’re not gonna dwell on it. We’ll find some way to lighten your mood a bit.

Clear but not patronizing

We explain complicated things in the easiest way humanly possible, but we do it without talking down or over-explaining. When someone reads something by Canopy they think: “Cool. That makes sense.” Not: “No kidding.”

Enthusiastic but not annoying

We’re optimistic and excited about our product, but we’re not going to jump up and down and scream about it.
