Containers

Purpose/Usage

Containers are designed to be used for grouping like content and interactions together. Some content and interactions are static - they are embedded in the display and are not moved. Other containers are transient, meaning they are used to quickly display information or interactions then disappear without disrupting the practitioners workflow. All of these containers are the standard building blocks of the app and are ubiquitous throughout the application.
